Remember the tones you create

Here at Music is Cool

We love to play music. Rock, Jazz, RnB, Rap, Punk, Metal, ambient, Shoegaze. Whatever type of music you can think of, we are down to jam. However sometimes when you're jamming you get caught up in the groove and music and forget different jams, progressions, licks, solos, rhythyms. So why not try to write them down so you never forget them. Arranging music is hard and it is especially hard when you try to keep it all in your head.

So with this Website we are going to try to allow you to record that amazing riff that you can't remember what key you were in or that bVI chord that shouldn't work in your progrssion but sounds amazing.
